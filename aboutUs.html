About Us

What is Schicksal?

Schicksal is an organization founded in the seventh century to research and combat Honkai. Three main families—Apokalypse, Kaslana, and Schariac—come with the Schicksal organizational structure in its long-term evolution as the core has progressively developed. Schicksal has built fortresses all around the planet to watch and battle the Honkai.

Structure of Schicksal

The Kaslana Family

The primary responsibilities of the family that oversees the "Knight" position are defense of Schicksal and opposition to the Honkai army. Siegfried Kaslana is the Patriarch of the modern day. Because the Kaslana family is highly adapted to Honkai, it is also used in numerous hazardous combat missions in the Honkai area. The Kaslana family members are forceful advocates with distinct social classes, therefore they are strong enough to protect Schicksal.

The Schariac Family

The household in charge of the Honkai's sealing, the "Divine Maiden" duty. The Schariac family's female members are revered as saints. The saints may utilize the energy of the Honkai to bolster themselves since they have the capacity to control it, but this manipulation power has a cost. The saints' destiny is to deteriorate and ultimately perish.

The Apocalypse Family

The Apocalypse that govern Schicksal completely lack strength and power to fight against the Honkai, unlike the Schariac or the Kaslana., and instead only govern the organization and facilitate its activities with their intelligence. Their role in Schicksal is the "Overseer".

Valkyries

Valkyries are strong female warriors from Schicksal with either synthetic or natural stigmata and a great resistance to Honkai. The Valkyries are organized into ranks: S, A, B, C, and D. The S rank is reserved for the most powerful Valkyries, while those ranked B and above are deployed to the frontlines. Valkyries with a D rank remain at headquarters, handling chores, support, and logistical duties, away from direct combat. There is also a special Paladin rank, designated for the personal guard of the Schicksal Overseer. Additionally, Valkyries are assigned to squads for various missions.

Knights

While men rarely possess Honkai adaptability, it does happen occasionally, and these men have the potential to become the Knights of Schicksal.
